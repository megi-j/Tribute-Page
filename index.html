Steve Jobs

The man who changed the world

Here's a time line of Jobs' life:

  • 1955 - Steve Jobs was born on February 24 in San Francisco, California and was adopted by Paul and Clara jobs
  • 1972 - He attended Homestead High School in Cupertino California and went to Reed College in Portland Oregon but dropped out after only one semester
  • 1976 - Wozniak(Jobs' friend) was building his own computer and Jobs pre-sold 50 of the as-yet unmade computers to a local store and managed to buy the components on credit solely on the strength of the order, enabling them to build the Apple I without any funding at all.
  • 1977 - The Apple II followed and the company Apple Computer was formed shortly afterwards.
  • 1985 - Jobs was fired from Apple and immediately founded another computer company, NeXT.
  • 1986 - Jobs bought The Computer Graphics Group from Lucasfilm.
  • 1996 - Apple bought NeXT and Jobs returned to Apple, becoming its CEO.
  • 1998 - With the help of British-born industrial designer Jonathan Ive, Jobs brought his own aesthetic philosophy back to the ailing company and began to turn its fortunes around with the release of the iMac.
  • 2001 - The company's MP3 player, the iPod, followed in 2001
  • 2007 - The iPhone launching
  • 2010 - The iPad launching
  • 2003 - Jobs was diagnosed with pancreatic cancer
  • 2011 - Dies at the age of 56
